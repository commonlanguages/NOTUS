Some things I can do for you

Dig for gems

I love disentangling things, parsing through dense information from disparate sources, producing a clear summary and identifying any conflicting elements. Whether you are trying to interpret complex regulations, wade through multiple processes, or find the root cause of a problem, I can help you uncover some hidden truth.

Find a purpose

I can help you problem solve by revisiting intent or purpose, which often leads to clarifying scope and requirements. You will find a renewed sense of energy when it becomes clear what exactly is the target.

Operate the things

When it comes to getting things done, there are many battle-tested tools and models to help sail the ship. I'm also fluent in "human", which really helps because the human element in a system can evade most software and process controls and lead to non-ideal outcomes.

Check your results

You'll want to measure the outcomes to see if you got what you needed. Effectiveness is the key here, and having these answers will feed into future iterations to help you continuously improve.
