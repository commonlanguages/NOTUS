Intro

I am a Senior Policy Analyst working on the COVID-19 pandemic response in Ontario, Canada. My work has helped hundreds of thousands of individuals get access to testing during the most intense periods of the public health crisis, helping them determine if they can safely continue on with work, school, and see their loved ones. Now in 2022, the current mission is to improve the biosecurity infrastructure and enable better response to future pandemics.
